# MindMatch Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phase 1: Lisbon Launch (Months 1-3)

### Week 1-2: Practitioner Acquisition

**Target: 30 verified psychologists**

#### Channels

1. **Direct Outreach**
   - Scrape OPP (Ordem dos Psicólogos) directory for Lisbon
   - LinkedIn outreach to private practice psychologists
   - Email campaign: "Get free visibility for your practice"

2. **Partnerships**
   - ISPA (Instituto Superior de Psicologia Aplicada) - alumni network
   - Nova SBE Psychology department contacts
   - Clínicas de psicologia in Lisbon (as backup referral source)

3. **Referrals**
   - Ask early adopters to refer colleagues
   - Offer premium feature for 3 months for referrals

#### Value Proposition to Practitioners

```
"Free patient pipeline for your practice"

- No setup cost
- No monthly fee
- You only pay 15% when you get a paying patient
- We send you pre-qualified patients looking for your specialty
- You control your availability
```

#### Onboarding Flow
1. Sign up (5 min)
2. Verify OPP registration (automatic check)
3. Complete profile (10 min)
4. Set availability (5 min)
5. Go live within 24h

### Week 3-4: Patient Waitlist

**Target: 200 waitlist signups**

#### Landing Page Strategy

**Headline**: "Find your ideal psychologist in Lisbon"

**Subhead**: "AI-powered matching. Local practitioners. Risk-free sessions."

**CTA**: "Join the waitlist - launching February 2026"

#### Traffic Sources

1. **Organic (Free)**
   - SEO-optimized blog posts:
     - "Como escolher um psicólogo em Lisboa"
     - "Quanto custa terapia em Portugal"
     - "Tipos de terapia: qual é a melhor para mim"
   - Reddit: r/portugal, r/lisboa (helpful posts, not ads)
   - Facebook groups: Mental health Portugal, Expats Lisboa

2. **Paid (€500 budget)**
   - Instagram/Facebook ads targeting:
     - Age 25-45
     - Lisbon metro
     - Interests: self-improvement, mental health, psychology
   - Google Ads (low budget test):
     - "psicólogo lisboa"
     - "terapia online portugal"

3. **Referral Loop**
   - "Get 1 free AI match when you refer 3 friends"
   - Shareable waitlist link with tracking

### Week 5-6: Soft Launch

**Target: 20 completed bookings**

#### Launch Sequence

1. **Day 1**: Email waitlist with early access
2. **Day 2**: Post on personal LinkedIn (Tomás network)
3. **Day 3**: Nova SBE alumni Slack/WhatsApp
4. **Day 7**: Open to public

#### Launch Offer

"Founding Patient Special"

- First 50 bookings: 20% off any package
- Free AI Deep Match (normally €9.99)

1. **Blog (SEO)**

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Doctoralia
- We're mental health ONLY (specialized)
- AI matching (they don't have)
- Package pricing (not per-session)

### vs. BetterHelp
- Local practitioners (not anonymous therapists)
- In-person option
- Community-focused

### vs. Direct Google Search
- Curated, verified practitioners
- Reviews from real patients
- Easy booking + payment
